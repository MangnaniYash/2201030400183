Exercise to Maintain weight
  1. Swimming: Swimming is a low-impact, full-body exercise that engages multiple muscle groups and supports weight maintenance.
  1. Yoga: Yoga is a holistic practice combining physical postures (asanas) and breath control to enhance flexibility, strength, and mental well-being, promoting harmony between mind and body. Through mindful movement and meditation, it fosters relaxation and stress reduction.
